About

fiwse2014 (fantasy international winter sporting event 2014) is a salary-cap fantasy sports game based around a popular quadrennial cold-weather sports competition.

Instructions

Participating nations have been assigned values based on past competition performance. Choose 5 nations, whose combined cost does not exceed $100.00 for your team. Each country earns points according to the following formula:

(300*gold medals + 200*silver medals + 100*bronze medals) / number of competing athletes

Your team can be updated at any time up to February 6, 2014 (UTC) at which point all rosters will be locked.

Point totals and standings will be updated regularly during the competition with the team(s) achieving the highest point total at the end of the competition being declared victorious and maybe winning certificates of accomplishment.
